PITCHING KEEPS US SHARP, BOLD, INVENTIVE

It's adrenaline, challenge, and craft.

 

It pushes us to rethink everything, break the mold, and make something bold.

 

When it works, it's beautiful.

These days, pitching feels like an act of faith—it struggles not because of creativity, but because the structure prevents real collaboration.

BUT FOR THAT

TO HAPPEN,

The playing field needs some Rules.

Responsibility.

Accountability.

Culture.

Equity.

Fair play.

Integrity.

If the system is broken, complaining will not fix it.

action will

We can break the mold and rebuild it together.

The pitching process can be a space of trust and collaboration where everyone involved has the conditions to thrive.

 

We educate. We create culture. We build value.

Because when the work is strong and the client is satisfied,

we all win.

What's the ultimate goal?

No, no, isn't to eliminate pitching, but to change how it's done. Making the process fairer , clearer , and more respectful so the best ideas can rise.

GET INVOLVED

is an international initiative to protect creative work at every level.

The purpose is to foster a culture of professional respect between clients, agencies , studios , and freelancers through a system of good practices, open tools, and eventually ethical certification.

What do we stand for?

Just fairness, clarity, sustainable timelines, real compensation.

 

Creative ideas emerge from trust, not urgency.

 

Creativity is not a favor.

It is work, yes, it is work no joke.

Is a practical ecosystem of good practices, open for anyone to share, translate, and customize.

MORE IMPORTANT: IT'S AN OPEN TOOL.

This manifesto is ours.

 

A container of values and resources for the entire creative ecosystem: studios, freelancers, agencies, and brands.

 

It is built together, not top down.

 

Written by real people in the industry, but open to anyone willing to make pitching fairer and healthier.

 

As the community grows, so do the tools.

 

Anyone can become an ambassador for a new way of

working.
